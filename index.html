ABOUT THE DATABASE

The Photographers Database is a tool for photographers, visual artists, art historians and art lovers in general, intended to serve as reference and study material. It will remain free for everyone for as long as it exists.

This database is manually curated by me, Nacho Dramis, a photographer from Argentina specialized in stage, portrait and street photography. As such, the data in here is highly subjective and prone to errors.

If this website helped you in any way, you can help me back with a donation via PayPal (link) or buying something from my RedBubble Store

Want to help but can spare no money? Share this page, subscribe to my newsletter Focus either in english or spanish versions, follow me on Instagram or Behance

Thank you for using this website, enjoy your stay!
